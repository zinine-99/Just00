Our Success Story

With ten manufacturing, construction, and trade enterprises, JUST Business Group PLC is a diverse business company in Ethiopia. It was established to fully file local and international needs on several filds with a presence in East Africa. Dire Dawa, Ethiopia serves as the home office for JUST Business GroupPLC. In recent years, JUST Business GroupPLC has established itself as the market leader in its food complex and manufacturing utility sectors, focusing on the construction of extensive infrastructure in Dire Dawa that is benchmarked to international standards. With four highly rated cuisine and real estate firms.
Just Business Group basic philosophy of "Growth Together," a guiding principle for sustainable growth, has been accountable for the company's achievements and leadership position. By realigning its businesses with a focus on climate protection and expanding community outreach through its environmental program founded on the concepts of sustainability, diversity, and shared values, we are committed to reducing its environmental footprint.
